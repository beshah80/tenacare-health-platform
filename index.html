About TenaCare

TenaCare is Ethiopia's premier health and wellness platform, connecting patients with trusted healthcare providers across the country. Founded in 2025, we've been bridging the gap between traditional and modern healthcare.

Our mission is to make quality healthcare accessible to all Ethiopians, regardless of location or language. We partner with hospitals, clinics, traditional healers, and healthcare professionals to provide comprehensive care options that respect both modern medicine and traditional healing practices.
